FINANCING YOUR AUCTION PURCHASE

First time buyers to the auction market are often under the impression that in order to purchase a property at auction they need to pay the full purchase price in cash and that buying on auction is riddled with unforeseen expenses. This is absolutely untrue. A buyer, after bidding and the acceptance of the offer, is free to apply for bonds or finance in the normal and approved fashion and many of our buyers discuss this with their bank managers before attending the auction, in order to obtain a level of certainty.
